About Me

Information technology has been my career for 20+ years. I was recruited out of college by EDS - getting my feet wet in the industry as an SED (Systems Engineer Development program). Soon after I became a Programmer/Analyst and have progressed into various roles including Development Team Lead, Senior Systems Analyst, Senior Business Analyst, Senior Project Manager (PMP) and Certified Scrum Master (CSM) - engaged with large consulting firms including Perot Systems, AT&T and IBM. My education includes a Bachelor of Science Computer Information Systems from DeVry Institute of Technology and a Master of Science Digital Forensics from the University of Central Florida. I have also completed the University of Central Florida's Full Stack Developer coding bootcamp.
